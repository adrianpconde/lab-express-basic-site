This is a tribute to Din Djarin... Also known as Mando or The Mandalorian.
He's a tough, resourceful, and efficient warrior, the titular protagonist of the Disney+ television series The Mandalorian, based on Star Wars franchise.
The character is portrayed by the Chilean and American actor Pedro Pascal (who also played significant roles in 'Game of Thrones', 'Narcos' and the upcoming TV series 'The Last of Us').
